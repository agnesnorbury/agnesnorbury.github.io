Agnes Norbury

My fundamental research interest is in elucidating neural mechanisms underlying motivational and decision-making processes implicated in clinically relevant aspects of human cognition. In particular, I am interested in identifying the brain basis of cognitive vulnerability factors for psychological disorders (addictive and affective disorders).

I am currently based at the Computational and Biological Learning Laboratory at the University of Cambridge, where I work with Dr. Ben Seymour, building computational models of individual differences in aversive learning processes relevant to chronic pain and anxiety.
