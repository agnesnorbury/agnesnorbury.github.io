Agnes Norbury

I am a research scientist interested in cognitive processes relevant to the experience of mental health problems such as low mood, anxiety, and post-traumatic stress. In particular, my work has focused on using mathematical models to better understand the learning and decision-making processes that underlie these ways of thinking – and building games to try and measure them.
